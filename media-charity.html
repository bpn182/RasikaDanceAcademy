Press Recognition & Media Coverage

Celebrating the artistic excellence and cultural impact of Rasika Dance Academy through prestigious awards and media recognition.

2011 Award

Kshetram – A Spiritual Ballet

Excellence in Thematic Choreography

A powerful Bharatanatyam ballet journey through South India's sacred temples, weaving mythology, devotion, and vibrant artistry. Featuring elaborate choreography and live classical music, supporting AIM for Seva's education initiatives.

View Press Coverage
2015 Award

Sri Chakra: When Gods Dance

Artistic Excellence in Classical Choreography

A grand Bharatanatyam ballet blending feminine divine (Sri) and Vishnu's ten incarnations (Chakra), featuring elegant technique, powerful abhinaya, and masterful stagecraft while supporting Northwest Disability Services.

View Press Coverage

The Sacred Grahas (2014)

Creative Hindu Cosmology Interpretation

Bringing the cosmic stories of nine planets in Hindu mythology to life through innovative Bharatanatyam choreography, expressive storytelling, and vibrant humor, supporting OZINDCARE through art and philanthropy.

2016 Special Recognition

Katha Vismaya – Tales of Wonder

Mythological Storytelling & Multicultural Innovation

A visually stunning Bharatanatyam production exploring feminine strength through stories of Sita, Radha, and Draupadi, followed by a vivid narrative journey across India's mythological landscape. The show concluded with a powerful message of unity and cultural harmony, with proceeds supporting the Resourceful Australian Indian Network (RAIN).
